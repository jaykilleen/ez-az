EZ-AZ

MAGNET LAB

Co-op physics puzzle — sit at opposite ends of the table
by JAYKILL
Two robot scientists work together to guide energy balls to the target.

Player 1 (bottom): ARROW KEYS to move, SPACE to drop a PULL magnet (blue)
Player 2 (top): WASD to move, SHIFT to drop a PUSH magnet (red)

Pull and push the energy ball into the bullseye. Magnets fade after 5 seconds. You can have 3 active at a time. Score as much as you can in 2 minutes.

ESC to pause.

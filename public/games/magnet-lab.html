EZ-AZ

MAGNET LAB

Co-op physics puzzle — sit at opposite ends of the table
by JAYKILL
Two robot scientists work together to guide energy balls to the target.

Player 1 (bottom of table): ARROW KEYS to move, SPACE to place magnet
Player 2 (top of table): WASD to move, SHIFT to place magnet

Take turns placing magnets. Push and pull the ball to the target.
More accurate = more points. Score as many puzzles as you can in 2 minutes.

ESC to pause.
